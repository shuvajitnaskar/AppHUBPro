HELP CENTER & FAQ

What is AppHUBPro? +
AppHUBPro is a premium digital gallery where verified developers and administrators share high-quality applications, tools, and digital assets.
How to download applications safely? +
Go to the App Detail page and click the "Download" button. All files are scanned for safety, but we recommend using caution with third-party APKs.
Are all apps on this platform free? +
Yes, most of the apps and tools shared here are free for the community. Any premium content will be clearly marked.
Is my personal data secure? +
We prioritize your privacy. We only store essential account information like your username and email. We do not sell data to third parties.
Why does my phone show a "Harmful App" warning? +
This often happens with modified (Mod) or premium apps because they are not signed by the Play Store. You can check our Safety Report for details.
Can I upload my own applications? +
Yes! Registered users can upload apps via the "Upload" section in the sidebar. Your content will be visible on your profile.
How do I update my Developer Profile? +
Go to your Dashboard or Settings. You can add a bio, profile picture, and social links like Instagram and Facebook.
What is Desktop Mode? +
Desktop Mode is a toggle in the sidebar that allows mobile users to view the website in a wide-screen desktop layout for better navigation.
Why do I need to follow on Instagram to unlock the site? +
The Instagram lock is part of our community security and branding. It helps keep the platform exclusive for our supporters.
What should I do if a download link is broken? +
Please contact us via the "Customer Care" section or send a DM to @shuvajit_naskar on Instagram to report the link.
Can I delete an app I uploaded? +
Yes, you can manage and delete your uploaded content directly from your Developer Dashboard.
What is the "App Request" feature? +
If you need an app that isn't available in our hub, you can use the Request form to ask Shuvajit Naskar to add it.
How is the "Total Rating" calculated? +
Ratings are based on user votes. We take the average score from all users who have rated the application.
How can I become a Verified Developer? +
Consistently upload high-quality, safe apps. The administrator will manually grant the Verified badge to top contributors.
Does AppHUBPro host illegal content? +
No. We host tools and educational assets. Users are responsible for complying with their local laws when using any software.
Can I change my username? +
Username changes are currently restricted. Please contact support if you have a valid reason for a change.
What file formats are supported for upload? +
We support .apk, .zip, .pdf, .jpg, .png, and .mp4 files depending on the category selected.
Is there a limit on file size for uploads? +
Yes, basic users have a limit of 100MB per file. Premium contributors have higher limits.
How do I report a bug on the website? +
Go to the "Customer Care" page and fill out the bug report form with details about your issue.
Will my app stay on the hub forever? +
As long as the content is safe and active, it will remain. Inactive or broken apps may be removed after 6 months.
Where are my files stored? +
We use Cloudinary and encrypted secure servers to ensure your files are always accessible and safe.
What is the "Trending" section? +
The Trending section showcases apps that have received the most downloads and positive ratings in the last 7 days.
Can I use AppHUBPro on my PC? +
Absolutely! The website is fully responsive and works perfectly on Windows, macOS, and Linux browsers.
How can I support Shuvajit Naskar? +
You can support by following on Instagram, sharing the website with friends, and rating the apps you use!
